About Us

In Vietnam, the Lotus flower is the national flower. It is known as the flower of the dawn and is the symbol of purity, commitment and optimism for the future. That's the reason of why our restaurant named NLotus.

Our chefs are top chefs of Vietnam, bringing for patrons the best experience and great customer service. Since opening in 2016, NLotus rose to become one of the Gold Coast's top restaurants.

We have built a reputation among Gold Coast locals for exellence of its cuisine and the warmth of its staff. Its fully air-conditioned cocktail bar and spacious lounge area is also a popular gathering place for those drawn by its quiet sophistication and contemporary wine, beer and cocktail list. We now have an extensive refurbishment with the more space, the same team, same energy and the same great food & welcome hospitality.
